An operating system designed for the NHS

Secure, modern, affordable, open source, and free - it's the solution to the modern cybersecurity challenge, rising Windows licensing costs and creaking NHS hardware. Register your interest now.

NHSbuntu is a free, Ubuntu-based Linux operating system for the NHS. It's a secure, reliable OS that excels in both clinical and non-clinical NHS settings, and comes with the right stuff to be productive - out of the box.
