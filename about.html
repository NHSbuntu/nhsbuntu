✎ Manage Staff members

    We understand that the needs of healthcare differ from other types of enterprise, and have built NHSbuntu accordingly. We know that the NHS delivers healthcare in an unique way, which is why we've set up NHSbuntu to be more bothered with productivity than billing.

    Medicine, as a profession, has always understood the need to share information and learning so that the next generation of medicine advances from this one. We believe that this should include the software that we use to deliver medical care. Open source software is a way to share and build on the best, and provide every NHS institution with the right software to enable them to focus on care.
